11   Not that I speak in respect of want: for I have learned, in whatsoever state I am, therewith to be content.

Philippians 4:11-13

Content: The text inside the callout box is the content.

Padding: The space between the content and the border of the box. See the padding inside the callout.

Border: The visible line around the box, separating padding from margin.

Margin: The space outside the box. This callout has margin separating it from other elements.
