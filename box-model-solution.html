Box Model Explanation

Content area

The text inside the callout is the content.

Padding

Padding is the space inside the box around the content. This callout has padding applied.

Border

The border surrounds the padding and content. In this callout, we have a subtle 1px border.

Margin

Margin is the space outside the box. The callout has a margin of 10px separating it from other elements.
